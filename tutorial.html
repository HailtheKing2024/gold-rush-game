How to Play

Welcome to The Gold Rush Game, a fan-made remake of The Oregon Trail!

Objective: Guide your party westward during the gold rush era, managing resources, health, and morale as you face various challenges along the way.

Main Mechanics:
• Create a new save to start your journey
• Make decisions at key points
• Manage supplies and health
• Navigate different terrains and seasons

Tips:
• Plan carefully before making important decisions
• Keep track of your supplies
• Pay attention to your party's morale and health
• The journey is unpredictable—be prepared for anything!

This is a work in progress. More features and content will be added as development continues.
