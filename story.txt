Game start:
So you’re going to start in a city in california, and youre going to travel around the state trying your luck for gold. There are a couple of different people you could be, including:

The Gov. Worker(Noob)
The Banker(easy)
The Miner(Medium)
The Launder(hard)
The Peasant(Ur coked)

Beginning of game(Preparation):

After they load into the game and select their character, they get the chance to name themselves. They can also name their party that they take along with them. They start the journey by going to the latest shop and buying supplies. These supplies include:
Food Sub type(Beans, Corn, Rice, Coffee, Tea,)
Oxen,
Pickaxes,
Pans,
Water,
Mercury,
Ammunition,
Medicine,
Clothing,
Tents,
Rope

After they buy it, they can now begin their travels. Their goal is to get at least 2000 pounds of gold in 3 months or before they die for some reason….

Destination 1: Sutters Mill:

They will begin their game by traveling to Sutter's mill, the place where it all began. This will be a somewhat hard place unless you get there early, cause the gold ran out after the first couple of weeks. This place has a HIGH chance of burguraly
